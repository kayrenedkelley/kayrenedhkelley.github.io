Upcoming Events

September 1st - 3rd, 2018 | Starts 8:00am.

The Annual Cycle the Continental Divide Benefit

Entry Fee: $75 per person. Experienced mountian cyclers only.

Registration/Entry at the Helena Rodeo grounds starting at 6:30am.

This is a benefit course. All proceeds going to Primary Children's Medical Center in Utah.

The course will go from Helena Mt. to Wise River Mt. The first ones to arrive will recieve prizes donated by local businesses.

The course is comprised of both mountain flat trails. Three days will be given to complete the benefit. Due to the risk of bears this time of year, every rider will be paired with another. Wrist bands will be given to participants. These will be scanned at checkpoints along the way to ensure safety. Water will be at each checkpoint.

Be prepared for hot days with temperatures in the 80's and cold nights with some temperatures below 32 degrees. A tent or shelter and sleeping bag is required, all gear will be carried by the biker.
