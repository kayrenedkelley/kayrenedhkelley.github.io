Missouri Headwaters Loop

  • Located near Three Forks, Montana
  • Skill level: Easy
  • Length: 15.30 miles. Elevation gain 75 feet.
  • Average time to complete 2 hrs 30 min.
  • Features river, views, wild flowers, and bugs.
  • Kid friendly
  • Dogs allowed
  • Cost: $15.00 per person over 12 years old. under 12 years old $5.00

Swan Creek Loop

  • Located near Gallatin Gateway, Montana
  • Skill level: Moderate
  • Length: 20.2 miles. Ascent 1,800 feet
  • Average time to complete 3 hours.
  • Features views
  • Great forest setting
  • Children 12 years or older
  • Dogs allowed
  • Cost: $20.00 per person over 12 years old

Hyalite to Swan Creek

  • Located near Bozeman, Montana
  • Skill level: Hard
  • Length: 20.2 miles. Elevation gain 3,950 feet.
  • Point to point trail
  • Features a lake, views, birding, forest, river and waterfall, wildflowers, rocky and possible washouts
  • Adults
  • Dogs allowed
  • Cost: $25.00 per person
