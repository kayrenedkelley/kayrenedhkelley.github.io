Good Weather for Local Roundup and Branding

This weekend is the Local Round up. Area ranchers plan to round up their cows and calves for shots, branding, and healthcare. Local ranchers have learned the value of helping each other. According to rancher Kuinton Kelley this practice promotes cooperation and keeps good relationships between neighbors. Most ranchers in this area are small with less than 200 head of cows. There are three big ranchers and Mike Hodges owner of the Rockin N ranch sends his hands to help with the round up. The weekend after, Mike does his roundup. "It's worth it, the other ranchers and their hands come to help me in return". Mike is the only one of the big three to extend this kind of helping hand.
