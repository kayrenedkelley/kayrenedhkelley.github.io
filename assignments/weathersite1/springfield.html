This day in History

On this day in history the first child was born in the town of Springfield. A boy was born to Sheriff Hodges and his lovely wife, Mary, while the Sheriff was hunting the cattle rustler Maynard Bullion. Marry had gone to stay with her sister in law Jane in Greenville as there was only a few women in Springfield. Unfortunatly Marry passed away a day after the baby was born from complications of childbirth. The Sherriff was able to see her just before she passed and together they named the baby George. Jane was caring for a child of her own and offered to be a wet nurse for baby George. The Sherriff eventually remarried and was able to raise his children. George went on to start a ranch over in Franklin where his decendant Michael continues to ranch today. The sherriff was successful in capturing Maynard and he was hanged a week later, which just happened to be the first hanging in Springville.
